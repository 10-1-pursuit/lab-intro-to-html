Recipe

This Grilled Chicken Dry Rub is a flavorful blend of spices you can keep at the ready for when you need a quick chicken recipe for any grilling occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 3 teaspoons dried Italian herb blend - or Herbs de Provence
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 12 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dry ground mustard
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper/li>
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper

Directions

    Instructions
  1. Whisk together all ingredients and store in an airtight container at room temperature.
  2. To use, rub all over chicken that has been drizzled with olive oil. Grill chicken until cooked through and serve, or slice or dice and use for other recipes or store chilled in an airtight container to use later.
