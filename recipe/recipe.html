Description Of Jerk Chicken

Savory, Smokey, Spicy, and Juicy! This Dish Is Packed With Flavor, That Will Wake Up Your Neighbors.

Ingredients For Perfection

  • Chicken
  • Scotch Bonnet
  • Jerk Seasoning
  • 2 Tablespoons Dark Rum
  • 4 Green Onion Tops, Chopped
  • Kosher Salt and Freshly Ground Pepper

Directions For Perfection

  1. Make the Jerk Marinade
  2. Marinate the Chicken
  3. Cook the chicken, Simmer Marinade
