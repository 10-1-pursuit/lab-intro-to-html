Shakas Recipe

This is my method to make Jerk Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken Quaters(preffered)
  • Cooking Oil
  • Jerk Seasoning
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 serving Scotch Bonnet Pepper
  • fresh thyme
  • GreenPeppers and Red Peppers for viewability/veggielover
  • Pyrex Baking Dish or Cast Iron baking Dish recommended(if you have a grill then grill it up!!!)
  • fresh garlic
  • vinegar
  • lemon or lime/lemon juice
  • Season to you liking with additional seasiong
  • Adobo, garlic powder, onion powerder, cayenne,
  • when seasoning you can add browning for that nice dark color when complete and also jucieyness

Directions

  1. Make sure chicken is thawed out.
  2. Clean chicken with vinegar and lemon.
  3. Cut off excess fat and skin due to your prefence.
  4. Generiously rub jerk seasoning (Walkers is my Choice) all over chicken
  5. Add diced onion, fresh garlic, thyme, peppers, and additional seasoning
  6. Place in seal tight ziploc/container and let marinate overnight
  7. When ready to prepare take out fridge
  8. In your baking dish of choice add some oil so chicken doesnt stick
  9. Add ingredients from bag
  10. Oven should be preheated to 350 degrees
  11. Cook for about 20 mintues on each side covered
  12. Then uncover and increase oven temp to about 425
  13. cook till chicken gets a nice dark
  14. Then chicken will be cooked after an hour and a half with nice beautiful color
  15. Enjoy with Rice and Peas with a Salad and some avocado to tone down the spices
