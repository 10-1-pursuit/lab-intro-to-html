Tom Kah Ga

Thai Coconut Soup, or tom kha, is a classic Thai chicken soup recipe that’s so much more intensely flavored than most western soups, it’ll wake you up, tingle your taste buds and make your nose run (in the best way.)

The things you'll Need:

  • 4 cups Chicken stock
  • 1 can Coconut Milk
  • 1 tsp Curry Paste*
  • 3 tbsp fish Sauce
  • LemonGrass or 2tsp lemon peels
  • 1 tsp Chili Paste*
  • lime Juice
  • Cilantro
  • Hot Peppers

*Yes, you could make your own pastes, but we ain't got time for that.

How To:

  1. Put the chicken stock in a soup pot, along with the onion, ginger, stalk of lemongrass and sliced hot peppers. Simmer over medium low heat for about 15 minutes.
  2. Add the coconut milk, pastes, fish sauce, and chicken. Let it simmer for another 5 minutes so the flavors can meld.
  3. Add the lime juice, cilantro and salt to taste. Remove the lemongrass and serve.
