Kansas City Ice Water

This Kansas City Ice Water is a very refreshing drink for hot summer days. Be careful, though — it is easy to have too many of these.

Ingredients

  • Drink shaker
  • cups ice, or as needed
  • 1 fluid ounce gin
  • 1 fluid ounce vodka
  • ½ fluid ounce lime juice
  • ½ fluid ounce triple sec
  • 3 fluid ounces lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage

Directions

  1. Fill a shaker with ice;
  2. add gin, vodka, lime juice, and triple sec.
  3. Cover and shake until the outside of the shaker has frosted.
  4. Strain into a chilled pint glass with ice.
  5. Pour in lemon-lime soda to serve.
