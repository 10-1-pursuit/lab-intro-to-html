Lemon Ricotta Pasta & Spinach

Home Sundial Recipe

Description

Looking for a quick, filling, and healthy meal for dinner? Look no further!! This pasta dish is easy to make after a busy day and the cleanup is a breeze.

Ingredients Needed

  • Spinach
  • Pasta
  • Ricotta
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Lemon
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Extra virgin olive oil

How To Make It

  1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil.
  2. Add pasta to boiling water and cook for 10 minutes, or until pasta is al dente.
  3. While the pasta is cooking begin the ricotta sauce.
  4. In a small bowl, zest one lemon and set it aside.
  5. In a medium sized bowl, mix together the ricotta, parmesan, extra virgin olive oil, garlic, lemon zest, salt and pepper.
  6. Taste and adjust seasoning to your liking.
  7. Add spinach to the boiling pasta and cook for one minute.
  8. Drain the pasta and spinach, reserving some of the pasta water.
  9. Return pasta and spinach to the pot along with the ricotta sauce and reserved pasta water.
  10. Stir well until combined and creamy.
  11. Serve with a drizzle of olive oil, fresh grated Parmesan cheese and lemon wedges optional.
