Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sadnwich. It has the perfect balance of ingridients and looks great when made right

Ingridients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

    • Directions

      1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other on a cutting board.
      2. With the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side.
      3. On the right side, spread a 1/8 inch of layer of jelly.
      4. Carefully place the two halves together so that jelly is on top.
      5. With the sharp knife, carefully cut te sandwich in half.
