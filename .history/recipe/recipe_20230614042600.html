SKIPPY Grilled and Stuffed Sandwiches

Description

Melty, ooey-gooey, mouthwatering perfection. Upgrade your PB&J for a toasted treat you never knew you needed.

    Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons SKIPPY® Creamy Peanut Butter
  • 4 slices Nature’s Own® Butterbread
  • 2 tablespoons raspberry jam
  • 1 banana, sliced

    Directions

  1. Spread peanut butter evenly on 2 slices bread; top with jam and bananas. Assemble sandwiches.

  2. Coat 12-inch nonstick skillet with cooking spray. Add sandwiches; cook over medium heat, turning once, 4 minutes or until golden brown.
